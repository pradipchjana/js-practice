**The Goal**: Convert a temperature from Fahrenheit to Celsius.

Fahrenheit: A temperature scale where water freezes at 32 degrees and boils at 212 degrees. It's commonly used in the United States.

Celsius: A temperature scale where water freezes at 0 degrees and boils at 100 degrees. It's used almost everywhere else in the world.

The Math behind it
The formula to convert Fahrenheit (F) to Celsius (C) is:

` C=(F−32)∗5/9 `
